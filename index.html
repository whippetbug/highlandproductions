About Us

Created in 2020 by Sorley Gibb, Highland Productions is an entertainment group, consisting of a few friends who love watching and making films. Here we believe that stories are pivotal to each of our lives, which is why we've been creating short films with stories that can entertain and intrigue people. Our group is composed of a few great friends who want to make the best films we can.

Our Team

Sorley Gibb

Director

Actor

Executive Producer

Sorley Gibb founded Highland Productions in 2020. He had already taken an interest in acting, appearing in multiple Shakespeare productions including A Midsummer Night's Dream and Much Ado About Nothing. Gibb took a YFA masterclass on Screenwriting in 2021 while writing his directorial debut 'Lost'. After two years in the making, 'Lost' was finally released, to became a great success on YouTube with over 450 views. He has since worked on other short films acting and directing in 'The Room' as well as 'The End of Life', the former of which he co-directed with Izaak Hutton. In 2023, Gibb received an A (band 1) in Nat 5 Drama (exclusively Performance assessment) and is now working towards his Higher drama award. He has most recently Produced and Acted in a short film directed by Izaak Hutton called 'The Silent Scream' which is set to release on YouTube this October.

Luca Taylor

Director of Photography

Executive Producer

IT & Graphics

Luca Taylor has had a great deal of experience in business and camera work. In 2018 he co-founded, along with Sorley Gibb, a newspaper called The Sunday Gazette, where he acted as Editor in Chief for two years. He has had an interest in cameras and films for several years. He began working with us on 'The Room', and is lined up to work on future projects.

Izaak Hutton

Actor

Director

Producer

Izaak Hutton has had a great interest in acting for several years before joining Highland Productions in early 2022, where he started working on the film 'Lost'. Hutton has also been involved in several projects with the Theatre of the 7 Directions, guided by Laura Pasetti. After the success of the release of 'Lost', Hutton worked with Sorley Gibb on a horror film called 'The Room'. He also appeared in the film 'The End of Life by Sorley Gibb, and has most recently directed a short film called the Silent Scream with us, which he also acted in. He is also lined up to work on future projects with Highland Productions.

Josh Holdernesse

Actor

Producer

Josh has had plenty of experience in film and music over the years. He's an experienced musician, who can play Piano at grade 7 as well as other instruments. He joined Highland Productions in late 2021 and made his acting debut in 'Lost' during 2022. His performance was loved by audiences, who wanted to see more of him in future. We have Holdernesse signed up for more future projects.
